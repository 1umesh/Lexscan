Client may terminate this Agreement immediately at any time without cause and without any notice. Upon termination, Service Provider shall not be entitled to any compensation for work completed but not yet invoiced. 7. NON-COMPETE

Client may amend any term of this Agreement at any time by providing 24-hour email notice to Service Provider. Continued performance of services shall constitute acceptance of any amendments.
